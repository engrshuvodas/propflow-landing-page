+
Absolutely! PropFlow is designed with simplicity in mind. You can set up your account and start managing leads within minutes — no technical knowledge required. We also provide onboarding support, video tutorials, and a dedicated setup call for new users.
Can I use PropFlow on my mobile phone?
+
Yes! PropFlow is fully mobile-optimized. Our progressive web app works seamlessly on Android and iOS devices. Field agents can update leads, check their pipeline, and generate proposals directly from their smartphones — no laptop needed.

+
Your data security is our top priority. PropFlow uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption, daily automated backups, and is hosted on ISO 27001-certified servers. We are fully GDPR-compliant and never share your data with any third parties.

Yes! All plans include email support with a 24-hour response time. Pro users get priority support with faster responses. Agency plan customers receive a dedicated account manager and access to live chat and phone support during business hours.
